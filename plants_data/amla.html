Amla

Phyllanthus emblica (syn. Emblica officinalis)

Medicinal Plants
Author: Yash shinde
Published: 26/11/2025
Read Time: 5 min Read
Category: Medicinal Plants
Location: Pune

Description

Amla () ek chhota hara khattameetha phal hota hai jo Ayurveda mein sabse zyada istemaal kiya jata hai. Amla duniya ke sabse rich natural Vitamin C sources mein se ek hai. Isme antioxidants, polyphenols, aur anti-inflammatory properties bahut zyada hoti hain.


Indian medicine system ke hisaab se amla Tridosha pacifier hai yani pitta, kapha aur vata teenon ko balance karta hai. Iska istemaal immunity badhane, digestion behtar karne, diabetes control, liver detox, skin glow, hair strengthening aur ageing slow karne ke liye hota hai.


Modern studies ne bhi dikhaya hai ki Amla blood sugar control, cholesterol kam karne, liver protect karne aur anti-cancer activity dikhata hai.

(Key citations: Krishnaveni M., Mirunalini S., Phyllanthus emblica: A potent nutraceutical, Food & Function; Variyar et al., Journal of Food Science.)


Medicinal Properties

Antioxidant anti-inflammatory antimicrobial antiviral antidiabetic hepatoprotective (liver-protecting) cardioprotective immunomodulatory anti-ulcer anti-aging anti-cancer (research-based)

Common Uses

  • Amla juice for immunity

  • Amla churna for acidity relief

  • Amla candies for daily vitamin C
  • Amla tea for detox
  • Amla tablets/capsules for diabetes support
  • Amla fresh fruit for skin glow
  • Amla water for liver cleansing

Growing Information

Growing Conditions

Full sunlight, well-drained sandyloam soil, slightly alkaline soil acceptable, drought-tolerant, deep pit plantation, wide spacing, warm & dry weather preferred

Water Requirements

Low to moderate water young plants weekly; mature trees need very less watering except in dry season.

Temperature Range

2035C ideal; can tolerate heat up to 45C; sensitive to severe frost.

Harvesting Tips

Harvest during winter (NovemberFebruary) when fruits turn yellow-green and firm Use pruning shears to cut fruit clusters Handle gently to avoid bruising Wash and sun-dry immediately if making powder Store fresh fruits in cool dry place

Preparation Methods

Amla Powder

Cut fruit remove seeds sun dry grind fine.

Amla juice

Fresh fruits crushed filtered consumed immediately (rich vitamin C).

Amla candy

Boiled or sun-dried pieces with sugar for long-term use.

Amla kadha

Boil dried amla in water strain drink warm.

Chemical Compounds
